About Us

We are a small, family-owned Chinese massage shop located in the heart of Chula Vista. Our experienced staff specializes in traditional Chinese acupressure and foot reflexology. We take pride in offering a peaceful and welcoming environment where customers can relax and rejuvenate.

Whether you're dealing with muscle tension, stress, or just need a break from your busy routine, our massage services aim to bring balance and comfort to your day.

Our Services

Traditional Massage

Our signature full-body massage combines traditional Chinese techniques to release tension and promote relaxation.

Foot Reflexology

Experience the healing benefits of targeted pressure points on your feet that correspond to various body systems.

Acupressure

Ancient Chinese therapy that applies pressure to specific points on the body to relieve pain and promote wellness.

Couples Massage

Share the relaxation experience with a partner in our specially designed couples room.
